# Finance Sangam: Official Platform & Operations Guide

## 🌟 Introduction
Finance Sangam is a secure, community-driven fintech platform designed to bring transparency and technology to local savings groups (Sangams). This guide outlines the core features and operating procedures for both Administrators and Members.

---

## 🛡️ ADMIN GUIDE (Superuser Instructions)

### 1. Security & Access
- The Admin Panel is accessed via the **Shield Icon** in the top navigation bar.
- Only users with the `admin` role can view this section.
- **Action Required:** Change the default admin password (`admin_g_in`) immediately upon first login through the Profile settings.

### 2. Member Onboarding (KYC)
- **Feature:** New registrations default to "Pending" status.
- **Protocol:** Before a member can borrow or be seen as a trusted entity, the Admin must verify them.
- **Action:** Visit the "Community Directory" in the Admin Panel and click **Verify Member** once you have confirmed their identity offline.

### 3. Financial Integrity (Deposit Approval)
- **Feature:** To ensure zero fraud, member deposits do NOT update balances automatically.
- **Protocol:**
  1. Member submits a deposit request on the website.
  2. Member transfers real money to the community account (Bank/UPI).
  3. Admin checks the bank statement.
  4. If funds match, Admin clicks **Approve** in the "Pending Deposits" list.
- **Result:** The member's digital balance is updated only after real money is confirmed.

### 4. Risk Management
- Monitor "Trust Scores" of members. High scores indicate reliable savers; low scores should be reviewed before approving large loans.

## 👥 MEMBER GUIDE (Community User Instructions)

### 1. Getting Started
- Register with your valid Phone Number.
- **Security:** Ensure you set a strong individual password. You can update this anytime in your **Profile > Security Update**.

### 2. Making a Deposit
- Navigate to the **Deposit** section.
- Enter the amount you have sent to the community representative.
- **Important:** Your dashboard will update your "Internal Balance" once the Admin verifies the receipt of funds.

### 3. Borrowing (Loans)
- Members can request to withdraw funds as a loan.
- The system will calculate the repayment schedule and interest automatically.
- Ensure you have a sufficient "Trust Score" to qualify for higher loan amounts.

### 4. Viewing History
- The **Profile** page acts as your digital passbook. You can filter transactions by "Deposits," "Loans," or "Interest" to see exactly how your money is working for you.

## 📈 USE CASES

### A. The Emergency Safety Net
A community of 20 people contributes ₹500 each month. In 6 months, there is a ₹60,000 pool. If one member has a medical emergency, they can borrow ₹10,000 instantly at a community-approved low interest rate, rather than taking a high-interest loan from outside.

### B. Business Micro-Growth
A member wants to buy new inventory for their shop. They use Finance Sangam to borrow a small amount and pay it back over 3 months as their sales increase. Their "Trust Score" goes up, allowing for larger future growth.

### C. Transparent Savings
Unlike traditional paper-based groups, every member can see their total "Interest Earned" in real-time on their phone, ensuring complete trust in the group's management.
